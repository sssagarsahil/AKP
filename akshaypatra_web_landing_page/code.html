Our Legacy

Preserving the Hearth of Maharashtra

Akshaypatra isn't just a restaurant; it's a living archive of flavors that have graced Maharashtrian households for generations. Our journey began in the vibrant streets of Wagholi (Pune), fueled by a singular mission: to protect the purity of home-cooked excellence.

Every spice is hand-pounded, every grain is carefully selected, and every Thali is a curated narrative of our cultural wealth.
