Zero-Waste Traditional Cooking

We follow the 'Purna-Annam' philosophy where every part of the ingredient is utilized. Stems are pickled, peels are sun-dried for powders, and water used for washing rice is repurposed for our garden.

This ancient kitchen rhythm ensures that our waste footprint remains minimal while maximizing the nutritional profile of every dish we serve to your family.

Wagholi (Pune)’s Finest Farmers

Our vegetables travel less than 15 kilometers from the fields of Wagholi (Pune) directly to our kitchens. We pay 20% above market rates to ensure our local farmers can continue their chemical-free heritage practices.
