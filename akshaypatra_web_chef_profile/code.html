Behind every aromatic blend, simmered curry, and caramelized dessert are the hands of masters dedicated to keeping centuries-old family recipes alive.

Guardian of Spice Ratios

Serving her authentic recipes for over 40 years, Maushi is the soul of our kitchen. She hand-inspects every batch of whole spices and supervises the stone-grinding process to maintain absolute perfection.

Executive Chef

Master of Traditional Baking

Trained in clay oven arts (Tandoor) across regional food capitals, Raghav blends ancient slow-cooking slow-heat methods with modern digital workflow precision.
